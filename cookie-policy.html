Introduction

Welcome to Shanishashidharan.co.in (“we”, “us”, or “our” website). This Cookies Policy explains how we use cookies and similar technologies when you visit and interact with our site, what types of cookies we use, why we use them, and how you can control them.

What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) by websites you visit. They are stored by your browser and can contain information such as a unique identifier, site name, and some digits. Cookies may be “session” cookies (deleted when you close your browser) or “persistent” cookies (remain for a set period).

As explained by regulation and privacy standards, cookies are commonly categorized by:

  • Purpose — e.g., strictly necessary, preferences, statistics, marketing (GDPR.eu)
  • Provenance — first-party (set by our site) vs third-party (set by others) (GDPR.eu)

Why We Use Cookies

We use cookies for several reasons:

  1. Strictly Necessary Cookies
    • These cookies are essential for the website to work properly (for example, to maintain your session, remember your preferences, or provide secure areas).
    • Because these are necessary, they are always enabled when you browse. (GDPR.eu)
  2. Preference / Functionality Cookies
    • These cookies allow us to remember choices you make (e.g., language, region) and provide more personal features.
    • They can also help us improve user experience.
  3. Performance / Analytics Cookies
    • These cookies collect information about how you use our website (e.g., which pages are most visited).
    • The data is aggregated and anonymous; we use it to improve how our site works. (GDPR.eu)
  4. Marketing / Advertising Cookies
    • These cookies may track your browsing activity across different sites to help deliver more relevant ads or to limit how many times you see a specific advertisement.
    • Third-party services may use these cookies.

Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies on our website are provided by third parties (for example, analytics or advertising services). These third parties may use their own cookies to collect information about site usage, ad performance, and more. We do not always control their cookies; please check their privacy policies for more details. (Данаилова, Тодоров и Партньори)

Consent for Cookies

When you first visit our website, we display a cookie banner / notice to inform you about our use of cookies. For non-essential cookies (e.g., analytics, marketing), we ask for your explicit consent before placing them on your device. (iubenda)

  • Consent must be freely given, informed, specific, and unambiguous. (DLA Piper)
  • You can withdraw your consent at any time by changing your cookie preferences. (iubenda)
  • Until you consent, we block non-essential cookies from being set. (iubenda)

How to Control and Delete Cookies

You have several ways to manage cookies:

  1. Browser Settings
    • You can disable or delete cookies via your browser settings (e.g., in Chrome, Firefox, Safari).
    • Be aware that disabling cookies may affect how the website works, and some features may not function correctly.
  2. Cookie Preference Tool / Banner
    • If we provide a cookie preference tool on our site, you can use it to enable or disable different categories of cookies at any time.
  3. Third-Party Opt-Out
    • For some third-party cookies, you can opt out via third-party settings (for example, ad networks often provide an opt-out page).
